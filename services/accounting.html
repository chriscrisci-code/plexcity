Accounting and Finance

Financial clarity without the operational drag.

Automating and streamlining accounting processes while ensuring financial data integrity—for credit unions and the partners that serve them.

Our priority

Powering the credit union industry and its partners.

Discover financial clarity and control with personalized support and proven tools. Our team elevates your financial strategy so you’re always ahead—without building a large in-house accounting bench.

Accounting services

  • Review and document accounts receivable and payable
  • Prepare, document, and distribute invoices
  • Update general ledger journal entries
  • Document and account for fixed assets
  • Maintain and update chart of accounts
  • Cash receipts and deposits
  • Monthly GL and bank reconciliations
  • Monthly financial statements and report distribution
  • Statement of Activity, Financial Position, and cash flow analysis
  • Dues invoicing, tax payments, and expense reimbursements (ACH)

Finance services

  • Accounting system conversion and set-up
  • Corporate credit card and expense software administration
  • Electronic scanning and storage of financial documents
  • Online bill payment management
  • Annual audit preparation and participation
  • Form 1099 filing, tax return review, sales and use tax filings
  • Aged receivables management and collections
  • Operational and capital budgeting support
  • Cash flow analysis, investing, and investment reporting

How we get the job done

We use Sage Intacct for automated financial processes and real-time visibility, Vena for budgeting and forecasting, Zeno for expense management, and the WEX Corporate Credit Card Program for secure corporate spend.

Fully SOC and Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act compliant.
