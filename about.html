Our story

Formed in the true spirit of collaboration.

Plexcity was formed by the California and Nevada Credit Union Leagues, Maryland & District of Columbia Credit Union Association, and New Jersey Credit Union Association. By embracing cooperative principles, they created a shared-services cooperative to aggregate back-office operations.

The plan was clear from day one: serve professional associations, credit unions, credit union service organizations, and small businesses with expertise that understands this industry from the inside.

No one knows credit union associations like Plexcity. Built by leagues and powered by staff with extensive league expertise, we are your partner for back-office support.

  • 2015 Year launched as a shared-services cooperative
  • 36+ Employees dedicated to client success
  • $6M+ In annual revenues, more than tripled since launch
  • Dozens Of organizations served—grown from three founding associations
