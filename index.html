Who We Are

Plexcity's staff of 31 professionals is dedicated to providing back-office support to members and clients across our core business lines — IT Infrastructure, Accounting & Finance, Human Resources & Payroll, and Consulting. We deliver a strong operational foundation so our clients can remain focused on their stakeholders and organizational growth. Structured under cooperative principles and led by an experienced team of industry-specific, subject matter experts, we know the credit union sphere. We speak your language. We understand your challenges. We've been in your shoes.

How We Do It

Plexcity provides a high-touch, fully outsourced HR, payroll, and accounting partnership designed to deliver a seamless, "white glove" experience for both leadership and employees. We aren't an agent on the phone — we function as an extension of your organization, bringing experienced professionals, proven processes, and modern systems together into one coordinated solution. Maintaining these services in-house requires hiring multiple subject matter experts and investing in the systems needed to train, support, and manage both staff and vendors. Our coordinated solution comes with a single, predictable fee-based structure.

Why It Works

Shared services has been around, but what Plexcity provides is more than service lines. Our client-centered approach shows up through availability, communication, and walking alongside you through ongoing operational needs. We build relationships with our clients — we're invested in your success. This partnership enhances compliance readiness, strengthens operational consistency, and elevates the employee experience, so leadership can stay focused on mission-driven priorities. That's the multiplier effect.
